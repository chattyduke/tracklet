315.523604″ (≈ 5.26′) — real on-sky residual
Honest framing. 315.523604″ is the genuine real-frame number — reported verbatim, never rounded to flatter. It is dominated by the TLE-age along-track error: the orbital element set is 0.598 days old, and BlueWalker 3 moves arcminutes per second in low Earth orbit, so a sub-day-old TLE accrues real along-track slip of this scale. The frame was blind-solved with no position prior. This residual is NOT a synthetic-gate pass — it deliberately does not clear the 10.0″ synthetic M0 gate; the M1 definition of done is an honest, plausibility-gated residual, not a tight one. The AC-4.6 plausibility center used to confirm the solver locked onto the right sky was derived non-circularly (from 3 other same-night frames, never the target frame's own recovered pointing).

Where the residual comes from (largest first)

  • 1. TLE-age along-track (timing / exposure-midpoint) DOMINANT
    The satellite-truth is an SGP4 propagation of a TLE whose epoch precedes the exposure by 0.598 d; over that age a ~arcminutes/second LEO object accrues real along-track slip. The DOMINANT term on a real LEO frame.
  • 2. atmosphere / seeing / refraction
    Turbulence blurs the PSF and differential refraction shifts the apparent position — absent on the synthetic frame, present on every real ground-based exposure.
  • 3. real optics / PSF / aberration
    A real telescope+camera PSF (coma, field curvature, focus) is wider and less symmetric than the synthetic Gaussian, biasing the streak-midpoint centroid.
  • 4. unknown plate scale / distortion
    The true plate scale and higher-order distortion are recovered only approximately by the blind solve (no distortion polynomial fit), unlike the exact synthetic WCS.
  • 5. detector noise (read + shot)
    Real read noise and photon shot noise perturb the centroid; the synthetic frame's noise model is idealised.
